Your Relaxation Journey Starts Here

The Marigold Suites

With magnificent views of the farm- rolling hills of flowers and beautiful sunsets- each room is bound to help you unwind. Equipped with king sized beds, jacuzzi tubs, and netflix, these suites are a real treat!

Culinary Delights

come check out our many different dining options. Stop by our morning bar for a quick pick me up in the form of a latte, fresh juice, or even overnight oats. In need of a wholesome meal? Head over to Dahlia, our in house restaurant, for a deliciously wonderful experience. Don't want to leave your room? Order in and we'll bring the deliciousness to you!

Grab a towel and head over to one of our many pools.

Whether you choose to soak up the sun while floating in the water or to read your favorite book in the shade, you'll be doing yourself a favor.

Relax and Renew

Make sure to book a reservation at our nationally acclaimed spa. Take a yoga class, spend some time in the sauna, or get a massage- there are no wrong choices.

Adventure and Discover

While you're here to relax be sure to make some plans to have a little adventure! Check out Portland, a wonderfully funky city, or spend some time on the farm with a picnic or a hike! If you would like to book something in advance, use the form on our contact page!
