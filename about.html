Why we started The Farmacy

As a young girl, our founder Madeline Bird (pictured left), dreamed of being a flower farmer somewhere in Oregon. Flowers of every shape and size always brought her happiness and she wanted to share that happiness with the world.

As Madeline got older she saw a need for rest and renewal and realized she could dream bigger than just a flower farm. With a love for farming and holistic wellness she opened The Farmacy- a wellness center that specializes in reestablishing the mind-body connection through fresh food, relaxation, and fields and fields of flowers.

Meet The Farm Hands

Madeline Bird
Owner/Director of flowers
With a simple love for flowers and an intensely green thumb, Madeline has been farming flowers and making beautifully unique bouqets since she was 23. Eventually her small business, Bird’s Boquets, grew into what is now The Farmacy.

Luke Pajaro
Culinary Ambassador
Driven by his innovative spirit and passion for flavor, Luke is constantly creating new dishes to add to our exciting menu. As a strong believer in fresh ingredients, Luke works directly with Oregon farmers to locally source all the ingredients here at The Farmacy.
